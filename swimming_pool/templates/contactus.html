Help & FAQs
My pool is losing water fast—what do I do?

Turn off your pump immediately and check the "Bucket Test" method. If it's more than 1/4 inch a day, call our Emergency line above.

How do I sync my smart heater to my phone?

Ensure your pool hub is on the 2.4GHz Wi-Fi band. Our Tech Support team can walk you through the "Sky-Connect" app over the phone.

Are your chemicals eco-friendly?

Yes! We offer a "Purple-Pure" mineral system that uses 50% less chlorine while keeping the water crystal clear.
